Our Position on Copyright

Bippi Downloader respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users to do the same. We are committed to complying with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and other applicable copyright laws.

Important Notice

Bippi Downloader is a tool only. We:

  • Do NOT host any video content
  • Do NOT store videos permanently (automatic deletion after 24 hours)
  • Do NOT control what users choose to download
  • Encourage users to download only content they have rights to

User Responsibility

Users of Bippi Downloader are solely responsible for:

  • Ensuring they have permission to download content
  • Complying with copyright laws in their jurisdiction
  • Respecting the terms of service of original platforms
  • Using downloaded content only for lawful purposes

Filing a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been infringed through the use of our Service, please provide us with a written notice containing the following information:

  • A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized representative
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed
  • A description of where the allegedly infringing material is located on our Service
  • Your contact information (email address, phone number, and mailing address)
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the disputed use is not authorized
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner

Counter-Notification

If you believe that your content was removed by mistake or misidentification, you may file a counter-notification with the same information required for a DMCA notice, plus:

  • Your statement under penalty of perjury that the content was removed by mistake or misidentification
  • Your consent to the jurisdiction of the federal court in your district

Repeat Infringers

We reserve the right to block IP addresses or take other measures against repeat copyright infringers.
