Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Bippi Downloader is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Browsing history or download history
  • Payment information
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • IP addresses for identification purposes

2. Temporary Technical Data

The only technical data we process temporarily is:

  • Video URLs: Processed in memory only to facilitate downloads, not stored permanently
  • IP Addresses: Used temporarily for rate limiting to prevent abuse (not logged)
  • Downloaded Files: Stored temporarily on our server and automatically deleted after 24 hours

3. Local Storage

Your browser may store:

  • Age verification confirmation (stored locally in your browser only)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. No User Accounts

Bippi Downloader does not require user registration or accounts. All usage is anonymous.

5. Automatic File Deletion

Any video files processed through our service are:

  • Stored temporarily on our server
  • Automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Not accessible to anyone except the person who initiated the download

6. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external connection is to the video platform from which you're downloading content.

7. Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • HTTPS encryption for all communications (in production)
  • Input validation and sanitization
  • Protection against common web vulnerabilities

8. Children's Privacy

This Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of
